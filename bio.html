About

Minjoon Jung is a Ph.D. student at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Byoung-Tak Zhang. He is currently a visiting researcher at Northeastern University, working with Prof. Lorenzo Torresani. His research focuses on video understanding and multimodal AI, with a particular emphasis on on fine-grained visual perception and robust temporal reasoning for real-world AI applications. In the past, he studied at National University of Singapore (NUS), collaborating with Dr. Junbin Xiao and Prof. Angela Yao, and was a student researcher at SNU-NAVER Hyperscale AI Center, hosted by Dr. Jin-Hwa Kim.
